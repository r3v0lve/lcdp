Short Summary

Season four had started with the gang trying to save Nairobi who had been shot,
as she had been lured to a window. They thought about sending Nairobi out of the Bank
so that she could get the care she needed. But it was too dangerous so they took the bullet out
themselves! As she seemed to recover well she encouraged the gang to continue with the heist.
But she ended up facing the barrel of a gun once more as one of the hostages Gandia had escaped
and had been spying on them. He managed to track Nairobi down and ended up shooting her in the head,
killing her instantly! It was a very sad ending. Still not over it.

😔 R.I.P Nairobi 😔
